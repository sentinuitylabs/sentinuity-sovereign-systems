WELCOME TO SENTINUITY V2

1. Run INSTALL_PUBLIC_PAPER.bat
2. Add your RPC and model-provider keys when .env opens
3. Optionally run PERSONALISE_YOUR_TERMINAL.bat
4. Run LAUNCH_PUBLIC_PAPER.bat
5. Use STOP_SENTINUITY.bat to stop and safely maintain local databases

Public edition: research and paper simulation only. No wallet is required.
