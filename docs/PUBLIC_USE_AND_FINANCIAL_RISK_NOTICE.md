# Public-use and financial-risk notice

Sentinuity V2 Public Paper Edition is open-source research, education and paper-simulation software. It does not execute live-money transactions in its distributed default configuration. It is not a broker, exchange, custodian, financial adviser, managed investment product, signal subscription or promise of profitability.

Nothing in the software, documentation, UI, model output, social posts or examples is personal financial advice, a recommendation to acquire or dispose of a particular asset, or a representation that future results will match paper or historical outcomes. Digital assets are highly risky; software, models, data providers and networks can fail; paper fills can differ materially from live execution.

The public release keeps live flags disabled. A person who independently modifies the software to enable live trading assumes responsibility for legal, tax, licensing, security, wallet, provider and financial-risk obligations in their jurisdiction. Do not enable live trading unless you understand the code, can lose the entire amount deployed, and have obtained appropriate independent professional advice.

Project maintainers should avoid guaranteed-return, “proven profit”, personalised recommendation or “safe investment” claims. Describe observable capabilities and test results accurately, disclose limitations, and distinguish paper evidence from live performance.

This notice is general information, not legal advice. Requirements may change. Obtain professional advice for the precise way you distribute, promote, operate or monetise the software.
